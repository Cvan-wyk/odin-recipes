Pap recipe

Ingredients

  • 30 ml (2 Tbsp) olive oil.
  • 1 onion, peeled and grated.
  • 6 tomatoes, chopped.
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) Crushed Garlic.
  • 2.5 ml (1/2 tsp) Peri-Peri.
  • 15 ml (1 Tbsp) sugar.
  • 30 ml (2 Tbsp) soy sauce.
  • 15 ml (1 Tbsp) tomato sauce.
  • Black Pepper.

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil, add salt.
  2. Gradually stir in MAIZE MEAL.
  3. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon for about 20 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Mix in butter to taste (optional).
  5. Stir well until stiff and cooked through.
