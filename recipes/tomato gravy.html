Tomato gravy recipe

Ingredients

  • 30 ml (2 Tbsp) olive oil.
  • 1 onion, peeled and grated.
  • 6 tomatoes, chopped.
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) Crushed Garlic.
  • 2.5 ml (1/2 tsp) Peri-Peri.
  • 15 ml (1 Tbsp) sugar.
  • 30 ml (2 Tbsp) soy sauce.
  • 15 ml (1 Tbsp) tomato sauce.
  • Black Pepper.

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot and fry the onion until soft.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes, Crushed Garlic, Peri-Peri, sugar, soy sauce and tomato sauce.
  3. Cook the sauce over a medium heat for about 30 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes to ensure that it does not stick.
  4. Mix in butter to taste (optional).
  5. Season to taste with Black Pepper.
